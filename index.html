Jayme & Chance

Chance and Jayme met the day they graduated from high school in 2013. Mutual friends convinced them to go to their grad night event where they met early on in the night and talked until the sun came up the next day. Chance reached out later that day and scheduled their first date. They ended up going to dinner and movie, which became one of their favorite date nights.
On April 28th, 2018, Chance recreated their first date and finished the night on the West Seattle bridge lookout over the city, where he proposed to Jayme.
On August 10th, 2019, the ceremony and reception will take place at Chance's parents', Daniel and Julie Brock's, Whidbey Island home.
